concatAll
A pipeable, join operator
What It Does:
  • Collects (joins) individual, inner Observables and subscribes to each, in order and after the previous has completed.
  • If the source Observable is an array, emits each item in the array, individually.
When To Use It:
  • In the first scenario, you should use concatMap instead.
  • In the second scenario, emit, transform and act upon, individual items within an array.

    NOTE: Variants include:

    • mergeAll
    • switchAll
    • combineLatestAll
    • exhaustAll
