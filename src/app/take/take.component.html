take
A pipeable, filter operator
What It Does:
  • Emits only the first N of values, where N is a supplied parameter, then completes the Observable.
When To Use It:
  • When you want to force an infinite Observable to complete
  • When you want to limit the number of emissions to a preset number.

    NOTE: Variants include:

    • takeUntil
    • takeWhile
    • takeLast
    • first
    • last
